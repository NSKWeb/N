Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is myly.io's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

Information We Collect

Since we do not require user registration, we collect a minimal amount of information. The only data we store is the original (long) URL and the corresponding shortened URL. We do not store any personal information about the user who created the link.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of operating the URL shortening service. Specifically:

  • To redirect users from the short URL to the original long URL.
  • To prevent abuse of the service.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. Our service is functional without them.

Data Retention

We retain the URL mappings for as long as they are actively used. We may remove links that are inactive for an extended period or that are reported as malicious.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
